KIRYONG TECH, the leader in press die technology

Introducing KIRYONG TECH

KIRYONG TECH is a specialized company that has been steadily growing in the press die manufacturing field since its establishment in 1994.

We supply high-quality press dies to the automotive industry, and based on our state-of-the-art facilities and skilled expertise, we perfectly meet our customers' requirements.

Through continuous R&D and technological innovation, we maintain the highest level of quality in the industry and pursue a corporate culture that prioritizes customer satisfaction.
