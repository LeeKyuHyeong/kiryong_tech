Welcome

CEO Kiryong Kim

Thank you for visiting the KIRYONG TECH website.

KIRYONG TECH is a specialized company that has been quietly walking the path in the press die manufacturing field for 30 years since its establishment in 1994. We would like to express our sincere gratitude to all our customers and partners who have trusted and worked with us.

Today's manufacturing industry demands constant innovation amid rapidly changing technology and fierce competition. In this era of change, KIRYONG TECH continues to invest in state-of-the-art facilities and technology development, doing our best to provide our customers with the highest quality products and services.

"Our customers' success is our success"

This is the most important management philosophy that KIRYONG TECH pursues.

We aim to grow together as business partners, going beyond simply manufacturing dies. All our employees work as one to accurately understand our customers' requirements and deliver value beyond their expectations.

Going forward, KIRYONG TECH will continue to grow based on three core values:

  • Quality First - We will provide the highest quality products that customers can trust through uncompromising quality management.
  • Technological Innovation - We will secure industry-leading technology through continuous R&D and facility investment.
  • Mutual Growth - We will create a healthy ecosystem where we grow together with our customers and partners.

In this era of change and challenge, KIRYONG TECH will open new futures together with you. We ask for your continued interest and support.

Thank you.

CEO of KIRYONG TECH

Kiryong Kim
